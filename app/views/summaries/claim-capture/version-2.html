Changes

In this version, the landing page has been switched from Find a claim to Start a new claim.

The paused claim notification banner has been configured to only show if the agent isn't already in the middle of another task. This is due to confusion around what the banner was actually for, and to stop them losing the information they had entered.

The content has been tweaked slightly on the validation questions to make it more obvious that we're looking for what the claimant actually wrote on their claim form.

Some bugs have been tweaked in the dummy data as the wrong payment details were showing on one of the schedules.

Moved the scenarios onto the settings page to make it easier to work with.
