BPS-661

Clerical to system

Have payments upto and including today been made?

Changes made

Changed the content asking the agent if they have made all of the clerical payments to date. Changed from a Yes only style declaration to a Yes / No question to see if it was easier to understand what to do if all the payments aren't up to date.

Research notes

Agents were audibly reading the question displayed to assess whether all of the manual payments were up to date as a decision was required due to the yes/no option presented. The previous yes only option caused agents to skim over this question as no decision was required. The inclusion of instructions to take if the payments weren't up to date ensured that agents were confident as to what steps they should take before proceeding on BSP manager.
