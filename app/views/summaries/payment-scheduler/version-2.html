Version 2 notes

Version 1

The first version we tested had the column names changed to 'Sent payments' and 'Due payments'. We also did some A/B testing, using either of the following two phrases; 'Sent to CPS' or 'Sent to Central Payment System'. The phrase was visible on every payment that had been sent to CPS. For the examples where the schedule was being set up and viewed on the same day, the due dates of the initial payments reflected that set up date.


Version 2 notes

Version 2

The second version we tested had the column names changed to 'Payments' and 'Planned payments'. We chose 'Sent to Central Payment System' based on the last round of testing, however this time we made the phrase only visible on payments that had been sent to CPS but were before the due date. For the examples where the schedule was being set up and viewed on the same day, the due dates of the initial payments reflected a date 2-3 working days ahead of that set up date.
