Offline, batch process

First attempt.

Changes made

Prototype designed to test what happens when an agent tries to use the service during the batch process.

Research notes

Agents didn't understand why they couldn't use the system. The smaller text on the page was not read and understood. Agents weren't sure if the system had gone down for them, in general or was busy. Agents would look to me to 'fix' the problem as they were unsure if that was meant to happen. Time differences for attempting to retry ranged from immediately to 30 minutes. Agents need to know when they can use the service following the batch processing procedure.
