Clerical to system

When do you want to schedule to start?

Changes made

In this prototype switches out the 'which payments have been made manually' screen for a the 'when do you want to start the payment schedule to start' screen. This was to try and reduce the number of choices a user had to make in order to reach the same outcome.

We also added a log entry to make it more obvious it had been moved from a clerical process.

Research notes

The agents found the log entry helpful. They understood that previous payments were handled clerically and this prompted them to find the previous payment information from the payment card or DRS.

Agents understood selecting the date they wanted a payment schedule to start and this caused less confusion around due dates of previous payments as they were aware that payments would always be due on the anniversary of the death.

‘No payments sent’ was confusing as agents are aware that several payments have been made offline.
