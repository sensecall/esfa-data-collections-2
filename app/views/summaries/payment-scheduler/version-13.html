Standard to Higher

The rate 'change' button stands individually.

Changes made

Prototype designed to further research the standard to higher journey. In this version the interaction from the schedule screen has been changed based on feedback from Dover.

NINO required for schedule is: AB123456C. The backdated date is 5 April 2017.

Research notes

Agents navigated through the change options confidently realising that the rate was separate from the payment details as opposed to the previous confusion as to whether clicking change would only provide the opportunity to change bank details. They were aware that backdated payments were referring to the difference owed to claimants from the uprating.
