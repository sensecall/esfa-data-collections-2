BPS-944

Validation errors - Date before April 6

First attempt.

Changes made

Prototype designed to test what happens when an agent tries to set up a schedule with the date of death before April 6.

Research notes

Agents appreciated the date of death validation as a good safety precaution, especially as they are processing two benefits with different eligibility and payment methods at the same time. Agents did not find this patronising during the journey. However agents are becoming suspicious to certain elements of the testing in general as they are now aware that we can engineer system responses to contradict what we've told them to do.
