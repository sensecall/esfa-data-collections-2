Clerical to system

When was the last payment made?

Changes made

In this prototype switches out the 'when do you want the schedule to start' screen for a the 'when was the last manual payment made' screen. This was to test whether it made more sense to the agent to say what they did last than to work out when the system needed to take over.

Research notes

Asking when the last manual payment was made resulted in less hesitation from agents and they audibly checked the dates presented against the front sheet to confirm which ones had been made to then select the last payment.

As the front sheet shows a payment due date and processing date this caused confusion for some agents as they were not familiar with using the new front sheet at the point of testing.

Including information about previous payments in the log gave the agents confidence that they knew the previous actions on the claim without needing to use supporting documentation from other systems. Enough information was provided to allow queries to be answered and agents understood that planned payments were set up from the correct date.
