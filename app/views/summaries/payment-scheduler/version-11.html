Standard to higher

First attempt. 'Change' grouped with bank details.

Changes made

Prototype designed to test the upgrading a claim from standard rate to higher rate. For the sake of this prototype all of the set up a payment schedule features have been turned off, and are limited to find a schedule, view a schedule and upgrade a schedule.

To find the schedule, search for the following Nino: AB123456C

Research notes

Agents easily navigated to the change option and confidently clicked through the questions regarding changing the rate or bank details and whether all previous payments should be paid at the higher rate. Once presented with the payment schedule agents weren't always aware that the arrears had been paid for the initial and monthly payments, this was noticed eventually but it wasn't immediately clear. Agents were confident upon realising that the arrears had been paid that the amounts were correct and that all future payments were at the higher rate.
