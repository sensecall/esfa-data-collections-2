Clerical to system

Declaration type page with one button to confirm upto date.

Changes made

In this prototype switches out the 'when was the last manual payment made' screen, and instead just asks the agent to confirm that they have made the manual payments to date. This was to test a far more simple process, and also to make the build less complex.

Research notes

Agents did not understand the significance of 'Have all payments up to and including April 2017 been made?'. This may have caused confusion as agents were not aware that this date was dynamic, and they thought that April was used in reference to the month that the payment went live.

As they progressed through the prototype the payment schedule was understood, and at this point agents were aware of the relevance of the date used previously. Agents need to be confident that all of the appropriate payments have been made when they commit to creating a payment schedule.
